Tuna Mayo Rice Balls

A fantastic blend of savory and sweet mayonnaise and tuna.

Ingredients

  • 1 bowl of rice
  • 1 can of tuna
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise
  • Salt, seaweed

How to Make

  1. Prepare the Tuna
  2. Season the Rice
  3. Shape the Rice Balls
  4. Wrap with Seaweed (Optional)
  5. Ready to Serve!
